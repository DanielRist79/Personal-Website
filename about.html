About Me

Hello, my name is Daniel Rist. I am constantly told that they were glad that they were able to work with me due to my positive attitude and outgoing tendencies to try to give the best I can give. At this moment I am currently living in Edmonton, Alberta. Since the beginning I have always felt that doing somethings are just a lot easier when you have some sort of instruction. So, when I create a website, I always start by figuring out what I will need so I can build a concept to build off.

When designing a website to be developed for a customer, the first thing I do is to figure out their brand and then their needs. But I typically try as much as possible not to make the designs similar in design. Except that I try to make all designs as simple as it makes sense to go. I just love it when things are simple, it has always put me at ease.

In my free time I typically like to surround myself with modern art such as cinematic movies or just racing video games, mainly Shelbys.

But when I am working, I typically like to listen to older music or a few newer artists like Nickelback or Imagine Dragons. I would really like to get the eventual chance to get some buddies on a road trip to see Nickelback or Imagine Dragons play in person.

I have always been a builder, and I just enjoy building things, digital or physical, and saying I created something. So if you are looking for someone that is a simple person, and I sound like the type of person you are looking for, I would be thrilled to talk.
